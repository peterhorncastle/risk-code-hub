Confidence Badge Legend

Each claim in this hub is tagged with a confidence badge indicating the source and reliability of the information. Use these badges to determine the level of independent verification available.

VER Verifiable

Drawn from court judgments, regulatory publications, Hansard, FCA/SRA/FRC notices, or widely reported public records. These claims can be independently verified from authoritative sources.

EST Estimated

Derived from broker market reports, industry surveys, academic research; reasonable range but not independently confirmed by primary regulatory authority. Use as indicator but cross-reference with specialists.

ILLUS Illustrative

Worked examples with plausible but hypothetical figures for educational purposes; not based on actual policies. Useful for understanding structure but not market fact.

GAP Data Gap

Information not publicly available; cross-reference with a PI specialist broker before relying on this claim. Indicates an area where regulatory or market opacity exists.

Knowledge Cutoff & Regulatory Volatility

Knowledge Cutoff: May 2025

Professional indemnity is a heavily regulated, constantly evolving class. Key areas most likely to have changed since cutoff:

  • SRA minimum PI limits and AMTC terms: Reviewed periodically; current requirements should be verified with SRA website
  • ARGA/Audit Reform Bill implementation: Status and timing of Big 4 separation, liability caps, and PIE restructuring
  • FCA BSPS enforcement and compensation quantum: Final settlement amounts and claims resolution deadlines
  • AI-related legal/advice liability standards: Emerging coverage triggers, exclusions, and underwriting practice
  • Big 4 audit reform separation timeline: Regulatory and operational implications for PI insurance

Always verify current requirements with a PI specialist broker and the relevant regulatory body.

Data Gap Disclosures

The following areas represent known gaps in publicly available information. Before relying on claims in these areas, cross-reference with a specialist PI broker.

E2–E5 GWP Breakdown GAP

Lloyd's does not publish GWP split between E2/E3/E4/E5. Total UK PI market estimates (~£2bn) are from broker surveys and ABI data — not disaggregated by profession. Individual sub-class data not available.

BSPS Aggregate Industry Liability GAP

The £2–3bn estimate for BSPS pension transfer advice compensation is FCA-cited but the final settled quantum will not be known until all claims are resolved. As of May 2025 cutoff, significant exposure remains unresolved.

Big 4 PI Programme Costs GAP

Big 4 firms (PwC, Deloitte, EY, KPMG) do not disclose PI programme costs or self-insurance reserves. All excess layer data for this segment is market intelligence from broker contacts — not verifiable from public sources.

EY/Wirecard Final Settlement GAP

As of May 2025, EY Wirecard litigation was ongoing. The €700m+ claims figure is from filed pleadings — actual paid quantum unknown. Settlement is likely subject to confidentiality agreements.

AI Legal Advice Liability GAP

This is an emerging area with no established case law or settled underwriting practice as of May 2025. Risk characterisation, coverage triggers and exclusions for AI-generated advice errors are actively under development by PI insurers and regulators.

SRA ARP Premium Data GAP

Actual Assigned Risks Pool premium levels and utilisation statistics are not publicly disclosed in granular form. The "3–5× standard market rate" estimate is from published academic research and broker guidance — not from SRA primary data.
