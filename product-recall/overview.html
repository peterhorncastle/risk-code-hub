About This Resource

Product recall insurance protects food and beverage manufacturers, distributors, and retailers against the enormous financial costs of recalling products from the market. A single contamination event can cost tens of millions of dollars — and destroy a brand's value overnight. This hub provides trainee underwriters with the tools, data, and frameworks needed to understand, price, and structure this specialist line of business.

Navigate using the tabs above to explore each topic area. The Recall Database contains individual event records with cost data, sources, and insurance status. The Underwriting section walks through rating methodology step by step.
