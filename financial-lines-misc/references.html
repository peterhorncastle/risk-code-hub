Section A: Citation Badge Legend

VER Verified

Independently verifiable fact from regulatory filing, statutory text, or published data (e.g. Land Registry statute, ALTA annual report, Fed mortgage stats).

EST Estimated

Reasonable industry estimate based on partial data, interpolation, or expert consensus. Subject to wider confidence interval than VER.

ILLUS Illustrative

Worked example or scenario for pedagogical purposes only. Not a data claim; numbers chosen to show methodology, not predict outcomes.

GAP Data Gap

Known gap in publicly available data. Sourced from confidential filings, market intelligence, or expert interpolation. Flagged for transparency.

Section B: Research Note

This hub draws on publicly available regulatory filings, industry association publications, and news reports. Data on insured losses, GWP figures, and premium rates are subject to significant opacity — particularly for MIG portfolios held by lenders, FG run-off portfolios, and private surety treaty arrangements.

Knowledge cutoff: May 2025. Claims involving ongoing litigation use pre-settlement estimates only.

Scope: Focus is Lloyd's market activity and UK/US regulatory context. International jurisdictions (EU, APAC, Middle East) covered selectively where Lloyd's participation is material.

Confidence levels: All claims carry explicit confidence badges (VER/EST/ILLUS/GAP). Reader should adjust interpretation accordingly. VER claims are citable; EST claims are directional; ILLUS claims illustrate method only; GAP claims signal data limitations.

Section E: Data Gap Disclosures

GAP UK MIG Portfolio Size & Aggregate Insured Exposure

Lenders do not publicly disclose MIG programme details or reserve adequacy. GWP estimates based on interpolation from BoE mortgage statistics and LTV distribution data. Actual UK MIG market may be ±30% from published estimates.

GAP Aggregate Losses from UK Conveyancing Wire Fraud

No central database; CLLS (City of London Law Society) and Law Society publish only partial data. Title insurer loss experience confidential. Estimates of £50–300m p.a. vary widely; precision unknown.

GAP FG/Monoline Run-Off Portfolio Ultimate Loss Quantum

AMBAC and MBIA structured books remain in run-off with uncertain ultimate loss. Final settlement figures not yet available (as of May 2025). Historical claims paid understate ultimate reserve adequacy.

GAP Lloyd's-Specific GWP Split for FM/TT/SB/FG

Lloyd's Market Bulletin data does not disaggregate at this level. Estimates based on syndicate reports and market intelligence. Actual Lloyd's exposure may differ from hub estimates by ±20%.

GAP Construction Surety Claims Data Post-2022

Carillion, Interserve, and the 2023–24 contractor insolvency wave loss quantum not yet fully developed in public loss reserves. Claims are still emerging; SFAA data lags by 2–3 years.

GAP AI/Deepfake Property Fraud Quantum

Too recent (2023–24) for statistical loss data. Title insurer responses based on individual case reports and Land Registry alerts. Systemic impact unknown.
